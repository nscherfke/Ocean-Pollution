This page contains some of the fun games that we created to bring awareness to the issue of Ocean Pollution. If you look below, there will be a way to access the game along with instructions on how to play the game.

Ocean Adventure

How To Play

This game was created by Peter Lichtenberger. You play as a turtle. AD to move, space bar to jump, and shift to spring. Try to avoid pieces of trash and collect all the coins to win! Be careful: if you get hit by trash, you're back to the loading screen! Good luck!

Trash Blast

How To Play

This game was created by Vincent Zhou. You play as a turtle. Arrow keys to move; space bar to shoot bubbles. Pop all the pieces of trash to win! Good luck!
