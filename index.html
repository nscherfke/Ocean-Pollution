Purpose:

Our purpose is to spread awareness on the issue of Ocean Pollution. Our goal is that more and more people are aware of this issue, and begin to help the cause. This website allows you to learn about Ocean Pollution by reading the information that we have, along with playing the fun games about the topic. We hope that you donate to the cause or help to spread awareness.



Ocean pollution is affecting marine ecosystems. If you are interested in taking action, consider contributing to one of the following charities dedicated to saving our oceans:

Ocean Conservancy
The Ocean Cleanup
Ocean Blue Project
